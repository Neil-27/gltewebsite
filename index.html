About Us

Great Lakes Tree Experts is run by owner/operator Conor Johnston, who is an ISA & Ontario certified arborist. Our team has the knowledge and experience to take care of all
your tree care and shrub requests. Conor has over seven years of experience in the arboriculture industry and has acquired a list of professional certifications and qualifications over the years, these include: Ontario College of Trades & ISA (International Society of Arboriculture) Member, ISA Certified Arborist, Ontario Certified Arborist, Working at Heights Certified, CPR & First Aid Certified, Ontario Chainsaw Safety Certified, and a Graduate of the Arborist Apprenticeship program at Humber College (With Honours). We focus on residential and commercial tree care, maintaining the health and structural integrity of all urban, as well as rural trees. We serve the entire Grey & Bruce Region, offering our wide array of services. The Great Lakes Tree Experts Team features highly trained and educated team members who dedicate themselves to the care and maintenance for the largest growing organisms on our planet.
